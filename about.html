About Collabify

A student-built platform for global research collaboration, aligned with the UN Sustainable Development Goals.

What is Collabify?

Collabify is a Global Research Collaboration Finder — a web platform designed to connect students and researchers across disciplines and borders. Users can create research profiles, discover collaborators through smart keyword matching, and post research ideas to find project partners.

Built as an SDG mini-project, Collabify addresses real challenges in academic networking: researchers often struggle to find partners with complementary skills, especially across institutions and countries.

Our Mission

To democratize research collaboration by making it easy for any student or researcher — regardless of institution or geography — to find the right partner for their next big idea.

SDG Alignment

SDG 4Quality Education — enabling access to collaborative academic environments.
SDG 9Innovation — building digital tools that support research and development.
SDG 17Partnerships — creating cross-border academic alliances and networks.
